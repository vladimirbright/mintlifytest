# Overview

Truv can be leveraged within any of the below platforms that you currently use.

![](/images/docs/43db08a57dbff618f06cb130bbaeb6b4e193fbfec3d179e42b7ba140bbea053f-Screenshot_2024-09-03_at_9.37.08_PM.png)

* nCino (fka SimpleNexus)
* BeSmartee
* Floify
* LenderLogix

Reach out to Truv team if the point-of-sale of your choice is not listed above.

# Key Benefits

* Leverage Truv products out-of-the-box.
* Go live in days, no implementation required.
* White label and customize Truv workflow with templates.
* Minimize change management.
* Gather all required data and documentation to accelerate loan process.

# nCino (fka SimpleNexus)

Launch Truv within nCino (fka SimpleNexus) point-of-sale system

* Go live with [Truv within nCino](/docs/ncino).
* Tailor and brand your workflow by utilizing Truv's Template functionality for full customization and white labeling.
* View Borrower experience within nCino.

# BeSmartee

Get started with Truv within BeSmartee point-of-sale system.

* Enable [Truv within BeSmartee system](/docs/besmartee).
* White label your Truv VOIE workflow using templates in Truv Dashboard.
* See [Borrower experience within BeSmartee](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SLDzqylL6ys\&list=PLfVIfYOibmGywDk6iq8_n6jUxh9GauaCu\&index=5).

# Floify

Leverage Truv within Floify point-of-sale system.

* Activate Truv within Floify using the [quick set-up guide](/docs/floify).
* Customize and white label your workflow by leveraging Truv Templates functionality.
* See [Truv in Action within Floify](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zDt_yeWaJ28\&list=PLfVIfYOibmGywDk6iq8_n6jUxh9GauaCu\&index=4).

# Lenderlogix

Utilize Truv's income and employment verification in LenderLogix point-of-sale system.

* Setup Truv within LenderLogix, [see details](/docs/lenderlogix).
* Personalize and brand your workflow by taking advantage of Truv's Templates feature.
* See [Borrower experience within LenderLogix](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AlUm1H9mIEA\&list=PLfVIfYOibmGywDk6iq8_n6jUxh9GauaCu\&index=6).
