# Evaluate your current process

Truv supports your users configuring their payroll accounts. You can then use that functionality throughout your process.

* **After opening an account** - Use new account and routing numbers to immediately update primary deposit information on payroll
* **For existing customers** - Create a streamlined process to transfer paycheck deposits to your bank
